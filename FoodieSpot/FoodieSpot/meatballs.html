Delicious Meatballs

Meatballs are a classic dish made from ground meat, mixed with various ingredients and spices, then shaped into balls and cooked. They can be served in various sauces or on their own as a delicious appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Marinara sauce (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a bit of oil. Cook the meatballs until browned on all sides, about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  5. Serve the meatballs hot with sauce over pasta or on their own as an appetizer.

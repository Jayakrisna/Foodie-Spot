Delicious Onigiri

Onigiri, also known as rice balls, are a popular Japanese snack made from cooked rice, often filled with ingredients like pickled plum or salmon, and wrapped in seaweed.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked Japanese rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., pickled plum, tuna, salmon)
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the cooked rice with salt while it's still warm.
  2. Wet your hands to prevent sticking and take a handful of rice.
  3. Make a small indentation in the center and place your filling inside.
  4. Shape the rice around the filling to form a triangle or ball.
  5. Wrap the onigiri with a strip of nori and sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
  6. Serve immediately or pack for a snack!

Delicious Pizza

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a flat, round base of dough topped with various ingredients, including cheese, tomatoes, and meats.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  7. Spread pizza sauce over the base, sprinkle with cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

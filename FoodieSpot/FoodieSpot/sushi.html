Delicious Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Nori sheets
  • Your choice of fillings (e.g., fresh fish, cucumber, avocado)
  • Soy sauce for serving

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice according to package instructions. Once done, let it cool slightly.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
  4. Pour the vinegar mixture over the rice and mix gently to combine.
  5. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving space at the top.
  6. Add your fillings of choice and roll the sushi tightly.
  7. Slice into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce.

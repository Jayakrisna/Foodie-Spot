Delicious Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a versatile and popular dish made from pre-cooked rice stir-fried in a wok with various ingredients such as vegetables, eggs, and meat or seafood.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, and green onions. Stir well to combine.
  5. Return the scrambled eggs to the skillet and mix everything together.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot!
